Our Story & Mission

About Radiance

A medically supervised aesthetic practice built on the belief that clinical excellence and genuine care are inseparable.

Who We Are

Radiance Med Spa is a physician-supervised medical aesthetic practice located in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania. We offer a comprehensive range of aesthetic, laser, and medical wellness services — each delivered under the oversight of a licensed physician and performed by experienced, credentialed providers.

We founded Radiance with a simple conviction: the residents of South-Central Pennsylvania deserve access to the same level of medically supervised aesthetic care available in larger metropolitan markets — without having to drive to Harrisburg, Hagerstown, or beyond. We are here to close that gap.

Our practice is not a day spa. Every treatment we offer is grounded in clinical evidence, performed with medical-grade products and equipment, and supervised by a physician who takes responsibility for the standard of care our clients receive. That distinction matters — and we believe our clients can feel it.

Our Approach

We begin every client relationship with a thorough consultation. We want to understand your goals, your history, and your expectations before we recommend a single treatment. This allows us to build a personalized protocol — a roadmap designed around your unique anatomy, skin type, and timeline.

We believe in conservative, outcome-driven treatment. We will never pressure you toward unnecessary services or overtreat to generate revenue. Our goal is to be a practice you trust completely — one where you always leave feeling confident in the care you received and the results you can expect.

We also believe your results don't end when you leave our treatment room. That's why we carry physician-dispensed skincare — Alastin Skincare and SkinBetter Science — and why every treatment recommendation includes guidance on how to maintain and extend your outcomes at home.

Our Values

Clinical Safety

Physician oversight is not a formality at Radiance — it is the foundation of everything we do. Every protocol, every product, and every provider is held to a medical standard.

Honest Guidance

We will tell you what we believe will genuinely help you — and what won't. Our consultations are educational, never transactional.

Individualized Care

We don't believe in cookie-cutter treatment plans. Every client receives a protocol built around their specific anatomy, concerns, and goals.

Lasting Results

We focus on treatments that produce meaningful, sustainable outcomes — not short-term fixes. We want to be your provider for the long term.
